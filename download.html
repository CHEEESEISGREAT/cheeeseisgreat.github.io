⚠️ Running the Launcher

Because Tobobs is an independent custom engine, Windows SmartScreen or your browser might flag it as unrecognized. Follow these steps to open it:

  1. Extract the downloaded tobobs.zip folder.
  2. Double-click your launcher executable inside the folder.
  3. If a Windows SmartScreen popup appears, click "More Info".
  4. Click the "Run Anyway" button that appears at the bottom.
